## Notes

- Dry-fit the controller board, DC jack, fan, and cable exits before tightening the case.
- Keep the USB port and power input accessible.
- Keep motor/endstop wiring strain-relieved so connectors cannot pull loose during arm movement.
- Do not close the enclosure until endstop and motor direction tests pass.
